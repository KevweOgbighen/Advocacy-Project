What is Climate Change?

Since the 1880s, humans have been the main driver of climate change. This is primarly due to the burning of fossil fuels like oil and gas. This produces heat-trapping gases in our atmosphere which leads to increased temperatures, the melting of polar ice caps, etc.

Climate Change Data

Global suface temperature has been on the rise for the past 40 consecutive years.

Sea level rise is caused the added water from melting ice sheets and glaciers, and the expansion of seawater as it warms.
